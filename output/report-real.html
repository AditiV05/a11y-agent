The guarantee

Detection and verification are deterministic. axe-core decides what is broken and whether a fix cleared. The language model only writes the content of a fix — the heading level, the alt text, the label.

Nothing is written to the live site. Fixes are applied to an in-memory copy of the page. These are suggested diffs: copilot, not autopilot.
